The comparison between Intersymmetric and Hold Space

The two artistic and sound-themed websites Intersymmetric and Hold Space both utilize minimalist aesthetics and emotional-driven web experiences, but the way they design interaction are very different.

Information Design

Intersymmetric uses an abstract approach in information design that users are presented with three different colored titles on a white background while entering to present different modules, there are limited instructions but have some patterns with simple labels to show the affordance, this allows users to explore through visual control options alone, creating an open-ended experience, but it can also make users feel uncertain about the purpose or function of the interaction.

However, Hold Space presents a clear and minimal instruction through the phrase “turn on the volume and press and hold the space bar”, which directly bring users to the interaction. This whole website utilizes the back and white style with simple line shapes, which this minimalist aesthetic focuses on the operational and emotional experience.

Mapping

The two websites applied different mapping methods. Intersymmetric applies a modular and multi-parameter system, there are various control elements like sliders, buttons and knobs, which each one reflects to different functions like rhythm, textures, and volume. This one-to-one mapping allow users to experience and provide precision, but is more design for people who understand music, but not transparent for normal users.

Contrarily, Hold Space uses singular and intuitive mapping, users only need to press the space bar to trigger the interactions with sounds and visuals, which people don’t need any foundation skills or technique, is a direct and simple mapping system.

Characteristic

The characteristic of Intersymmetric is functional and experimental, it utilizes warm colours to create a playful tone, which softness the technical nature. This website and the generative audio tool share the same aesthetic; therefore, it is more designed for people who have a basic understanding of music.

In contrast, Hold Space is based on black and white, and is characterized by simple and moving geometric shapes. This calming auditory experience and visual effect gives people a calm atmosphere, which designed for anyone who enjoy sounds and motion graphics.

Feedback

Intersymmetric provides functional feedback that when users adjust the above parameters, they can immediately get the reflects of sounds and graphical interface. Because there are no specific instructions, so the effective of the feedback depend on how users interact with the interface and how they control and interpret the changes.

Similarly, in Hold Space, when people press the spacebar, they can immediately see visual effects changes like background color and geometric shapes expansion with ambient audio, but Hold Space is more relate to immersive emotional resonance but not technical clarity.

Reflection

The interfaces of the Intersymmetric and Hold Space include different design strategies while express their distinct interaction goals. Intersymmetric focus on experimental sounds mastery through multi-panel layout and parameter-driven interactions to let the users can granularly control and explore their own creative experiences. On the other hand, Hold Space evokes emotional immersion through contemplative audiovisual experience, users only need to press a button to enter the interaction, this simplicity promotes emotional impact rather than productivity. Similarly, both websites emphasize immediate feedback, and users can receive visual and auditory feedback while taking actions. This contrast illustrates how interaction design and feedback mechanisms, combined with the intended interaction goals, can shape different experiences and feelings for users.
